# Lists

Lists are very useful for tracking information. They can come in two forms: Ordered and Unordered.

## Ordered

```
1. Item 1
1. Item 2
1. Item 3
1. Item 4
    1. Item 4a
    1. Item 4b
```

1. Item 1
1. Item 2
1. Item 3
1. Item 4
    1. Item 4a
    1. Item 4b

## Unordered

```
* Item 1
* Item 2
* Item 3
* Item 4
    * Item 4a
    * Item 4b
```

* Item 1
* Item 2
* Item 3
* Item 4
    * Item 4a
    * Item 4b
